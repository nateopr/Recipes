Pasta

Ah yes - Pasta! I could go on and on about how much I love eating pasta. When I was but a mere lad, I would look up at my dear mother, and ask her, "Mother! From whence cometh the pasta noodle, and until what time must I wait to partake in it again?" And now, I present to you, the best pasta recipe. One that was passed down from Mario himself after he and his brother took down the great green dinosaur. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles grown from an al-dente tree
  • Purified tears of the mushroom kingdom
  • Tomatoes plucked from the heart of an ancient being
  • Literally any green leaves you can find outside

Steps

  1. To start, place the tears in a pan, and boil at medium heat
  2. After tears have begun to boil, add the pasta noodles
  3. While pasta is boiling, smash tomatoes into a paste
  4. After exactly 5:47 seconds, strain the noodles. Do not let them get soft, otherwise they will come to life and fight back
  5. Add tomatoe paste
  6. Add leaves
  7. Enjoy!!
